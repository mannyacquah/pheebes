About the properties.

Pheebe Jasper Homes is located at the Pheebe Agate site, which lies on latitude 5o 52’N, and longitude 0o 04W East Peduase Lodge, Dodowa in the Greater Accra. The total area under construction is 30 acres.

It lies to the East of Accra, the Capital of Ghana; within the lowlands overlooking the Aburi Mountains. Enjoys the very cool breeze that may not need air conditioning in the night at some periods of the year. The area is generally flat with historic baobab trees, which are all preserved.

The site is accessible from Lagos-Accra ECOWAS highway off the Tema Motorway Round, approximately about, which links the Tema-Akosombo Dam high way. It is also reached from Accra International Airport by an Accra-Tema Motorway (Expressway), off Tema-Akosombo highway approximately 60 km (38 miles) from the airport and . You can easily drive about 40 minutes to the Akosombo Hydropower Dam, the largest man-made lake in the world. Alternatively, the site can be reached from the Accra International Airport by the Accra-Peduase Lodge-Aburi highway, also about 50 km (about 31 miles). The six lane highway is now under construction which will reduce the access time substantially when completed. Peduase Lodge is Ghana’s Presidential Retreat equivalent to Camp David for the US President. The site strategic location can thus be reached through an excellent road network within 30-40 minutes from three major centers in Ghana i.e. Accra, Tema and Koforidua.
